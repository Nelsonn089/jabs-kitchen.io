Welcome to GYAPS KITCHEN

We are a family-owned restaurant with a passion for serving delicious Ghanaian cuisine.

Our journey began 2020 in Akwatia at the Eastern region of Ghana, and since then, we have been dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience.

At Gyaps Kitchen, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients sourced locally to create mouthwatering dishes that will tantalize your taste buds.

Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch or a special occasion dinner, our friendly staff is here to ensure your visit is memorable.

Come and experience the flavors and warmth of Gyaps Kitchen. We can't wait to serve you!
